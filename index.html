    Our Mission

    Producing Decision-Makers Who Drive Change

    Collaborative, Interdisciplinary & Customizable Program

    It equips graduates with technical capabilities in data intelligence and analytics, critical thinking skills, and a strong theoretical foundation in education and social sciences.

    As communities require solutions that address complex challenges, this integrated approach offers students the knowledge and skills to not only collect, measure, and report data‚Äîbut to contextualize it, detect bias, and derive insights for responsible data-driven decisions.
