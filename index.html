Why Choose Our Program?

Our Mission: Producing Decision-Makers Who Drive Change

Collaborative, Interdisciplinary & Customizable Program

It equips graduates with technical capabilities in data intelligence and analytics, critical thinking skills, and a strong theoretical foundation in education and social sciences.

Responsible Data-Driven Decisions for Social Impacts

As communities require solutions that address complex challenges, this integrated approach offers students the knowledge and skills to not only collect, measure, and report data—but to contextualize it, detect bias, and derive insights for responsible data-driven decisions.

Interdisciplinary Approach for Actionable Insights

In a collaboration with various departments at UM, students will learn and integrate knowledge from education, sociology, psychology, and communication for contextualized intelligence that drives real-world impact and change.

Mentorship and Career Outcomes

Learn from expert faculty, collaborate with peers, and graduate with a strong portfolio, internship experience, and career support across multiple industries.
